The Reality

Know The Danger

Addiction destroys not just the body, but the soul.

Alcohol

Impact: Depressant shutting down organs.

  • Liver Cirrhosis & Cancer
  • Wet Brain (Dementia)
  • Withdrawal can kill (Seizures)

Nicotine

Impact: Leading cause of preventable death.

  • Lung Cancer (90% cases)
  • Hardens Arteries (Stroke Risk)
  • Gum Disease & Tooth Loss

Cannabis

Impact: Impairs cognitive functions.

  • Permanent IQ Drop (in youth)
  • Triggers Schizophrenia
  • Amotivational Syndrome

Opioids

Impact: Blocks pain, stops breathing.

  • Respiratory Failure (Coma)
  • High Overdose Death Rate
  • Risk of HIV/Hepatitis

Cocaine

Impact: Powerful heart stimulant.

  • Instant Cardiac Arrest
  • Nasal Septum Collapse
  • Extreme Paranoia

Meth

Impact: Destroys nervous system.

  • "Meth Mouth" (Rotting Teeth)
  • Feeling Bugs Under Skin
  • Dopamine Receptors Death

Benzos

Impact: Sedatives that cause blackouts.

  • Total Memory Blackouts
  • Deadly if mixed with Alcohol
  • Loss of Motor Control

MDMA

Impact: Overheats the body.

  • Severe Hyperthermia (Heatstroke)
  • Dehydration & Kidney Failure
  • Post-use Severe Depression

Inhalants

Impact: Starves brain of oxygen.

  • Immediate Brain Damage
  • "Sudden Sniffing Death"
  • Hearing Loss & Spasms

LSD

Impact: Distorts reality & perception.

  • Terrifying "Bad Trips"
  • Flashbacks years later
  • Accidents due to delusions
